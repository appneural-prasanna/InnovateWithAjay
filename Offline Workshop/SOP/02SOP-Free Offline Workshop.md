# **1. PURPOSE**

To establish a standardized process for executing a **Free Offline Generative AI Workshop** with:

* Structured execution model
* High student engagement through gamification
* Effective lead generation and data collection
* Strong brand visibility
* Conversion into long-term community and opportunities

---

# **2. SCOPE**

This SOP covers:

* Pre-workshop preparation
* On-ground execution
* Engagement and participation system
* Post-workshop follow-up and reporting

---

# **3. DEFINITIONS**

* **Registered Participant:** Student who registered
* **Attendee:** Student physically present
* **Active Participant:** Student engaged in activities
* **Top Performer:** High-performing student based on tasks
* **Converted Lead:** Participant joining community or future programs

---

# **4. ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES**

| Role          | Responsibility                           |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Trainer       | Conduct training + engagement activities |
| BDE           | Registration + student coordination      |
| Brand Manager | Promotion + branding visibility          |
| Operations    | Venue setup + logistics + data tracking  |
| Volunteers    | Student handling + engagement support    |

---

# **5. PROCESS OVERVIEW**

1. Pre-Workshop (Setup + Registration + Engagement Planning)
2. During Workshop (Execution + Gamification + Interaction)
3. Post-Workshop (Conversion + Community + Reporting)

# ⭐ **6.2.5 ADVANCED ENGAGEMENT & GAMIFICATION SYSTEM**

---

## 🎯 **Objective**

Maximize participation, interaction, and learning retention.

---

## 🏆 **Engagement Activities**

### 🔹 1. Live Leaderboard System

* Points assigned for:

  * Asking questions
  * Completing tasks
  * Active participation
* Top performers displayed during session

---

### 🔹 2. AI Challenge Rounds

* Round 1: Basic prompt task
* Round 2: Creative AI use-case
* Round 3: Mini project challenge

---

### 🔹 3. Rapid Fire Interaction

* Quick AI-related questions
* Instant rewards for correct answers

---

### 🔹 4. Group Activity (for large audience)

* Divide students into groups
* Assign collaborative AI task

---

### 🔹 5. Live Recognition

* Announce winners during session
* Encourage competition

---
