# **1. PURPOSE**

The purpose of this SOP is to establish a standardized process for planning and executing a **Free Online Generative AI Workshop** with:

* Structured lead generation
* High participant engagement
* Gamification-driven learning
* Community building and conversion

---

# **2. SCOPE**

This SOP applies to:

* Pre-workshop planning and lead generation
* Workshop execution and engagement activities
* Post-workshop lead conversion and reporting

---

# **3. DEFINITIONS**

* **Lead:** A registered participant
* **Active Lead:** Participant attending and engaging in workshop
* **Converted Lead:** Participant joining community or future programs

---

# **4. ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES**

| Role          | Responsibility                             |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Trainer       | Deliver session, demo, Q&A                 |
| BDE           | Lead generation, follow-ups, database      |
| Brand Manager | Marketing, creatives, promotion            |
| Operations    | Technical setup, attendance, documentation |

---

# **5. PROCESS OVERVIEW**

The process is divided into three phases:

1. Pre-Workshop (Lead Generation & Setup)
2. During Workshop (Execution & Engagement)
3. Post-Workshop (Conversion & Reporting)

---

# **6. DETAILED PROCEDURE**

---

# 🟢 **6.1 PRE-WORKSHOP PHASE**

### **6.1.1 Planning**

* Define workshop objective and agenda
* Finalize topic and duration (2–3 hours)
* Assign team roles

**Documents:**

* Workshop Objective Document
* Agenda Sheet
* Role Allocation Sheet

---

### **6.1.2 Registration Setup**

* Create Registration Form
* Capture participant details
* Maintain lead database

**Documents:**

* Registration Form
* Lead Master Sheet
* Participant Database

---

### **6.1.3 Marketing & Lead Generation**

* Promote via WhatsApp, Instagram, LinkedIn
* Execute daily outreach

**Documents:**

* Posters (Registration / Reminder / Urgency)
* Social Media Plan
* Message Templates

---

### **6.1.4 Lead Follow-up**

* Send confirmations
* Follow-up non-registered leads

**Documents:**

* Follow-up Tracker
* Confirmation Messages

---

### **6.1.5 Technical Setup**

* Create meeting link(Zoom/Team/Gmeet)
* Test audio/video
* Prepare PPT

**Documents:**

* Meeting Link Document
* Attendance Form
* Intro & Main PPT
* Backup Link

---

# 🔵 **6.2 DURING WORKSHOP PHASE**

---

### **6.2.1 Entry & Attendance**

* Participants join session
* Attendance captured

**Documents:**

* Attendance Sheet
* Entry Verification Sheet

---

### **6.2.2 Introduction**

* Organization introduction
* Objective & agenda explanation

**Documents:**

* Intro PPT
* Agenda Sheet

---

### **6.2.3 Training Delivery**

* AI fundamentals
* Prompt engineering
* Tools demonstration

**Documents:**

* Main Content PPT
* AI Tools List

---

### **6.2.4 Live Demonstration**

* Real-time AI use case
* Step-by-step execution

**Documents:**

* Demo Script
* Prompt Samples

---

# ⭐ **6.2.5 ENGAGEMENT & GAMIFICATION SYSTEM**

### **Objective:**

Increase participation and retention through structured engagement.

---

### **Activities:**

**a. Leaderboard System**

* Rank participants based on:

  * Task completion
  * Interaction level
  * Performance

**b. Live AI Challenge**

* Assign real-time task
* Evaluate submissions

**c. Recognition System**

* Top Performer
* Best Prompt Creator
* Most Active Participant

---

### **Participant Benefits:**

* Certificate of Participation
* Leaderboard Recognition
* Internship Opportunities (Top performers)
* Volunteer Opportunities
* Access to AI Community
* Priority for advanced workshops

---

**Documents:**

* Leaderboard Sheet
* Task Submission Sheet
* Winner List Document

---

### **6.2.6 Hands-on Activity**

* Assign practical task
* Track submissions

**Documents:**

* Task Sheet
* Submission Tracker

---

### **6.2.7 Feedback & Community Conversion**

* Share feedback form
* Invite to community

**Documents:**

* Feedback Form
* Community Link Document
* Resource Sheet

---

# **7. KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs)**

* Registrations: 300+(Assume)
* Attendance: 200+
* Engagement Rate: ≥70%
* Feedback Rate: ≥50%
* Community Join Rate: ≥60%

---

# **8. RISK & MITIGATION**

| Risk              | Mitigation                   |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Low attendance    | Strong follow-up & reminders |
| Technical failure | Backup link & internet       |
| Low engagement    | Gamification activities      |
| Drop-offs         | Interactive tasks            |

---

# **9. OUTPUT / DELIVERABLES**

* Lead Database
* Engaged Participant List
* Community Members
