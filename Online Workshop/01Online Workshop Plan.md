# 📄 **ONLINE GENERATIVE AI WORKSHOP – EXECUTION PLAN**

### *(Free Workshop | Lead Generation & Engagement Focus)*

**Prepared By** InnovateWithAjay
**Mode:** Online (Zoom / Google Meet)
**Target Audience:** Students 
**Target Outcome:** 200+ Registrations | 100+ Attendees

---

# **1. OBJECTIVE**

To successfully execute a high-impact online workshop that:

* Generates quality student leads
* Delivers practical AI learning
* Ensures high engagement through structured activities
* Builds a long-term student community
* Identifies top performers for internships and volunteer roles

---

# **2. EXECUTION APPROACH**

The workshop will be executed in a **3-stage structured approach**:

1. **Pre-Workshop (Lead Generation & Setup)**
2. **Workshop Execution (Delivery & Engagement)**
3. **Post-Workshop (Conversion & Growth)**

---

# 🟢 **3. PRE-WORKSHOP EXECUTION PLAN**

## **3.1 Planning & Preparation**

* Finalize workshop topic, title, and agenda
* Define session structure (2 hours)
* Assign roles (Trainer, BDE, Brand Manager, Operations)

---

## **3.2 Lead Generation Strategy**

* Launch registration form for data collection

* Execute promotions through:

  * WhatsApp student groups
  * Instagram & LinkedIn
  * Direct outreach

* Apply urgency-based messaging (limited seats)

### 🎯 Target:

* 100–200 registrations

---

## **3.3 Lead Management**

* Send confirmation messages post-registration
* Execute reminder strategy (T-3, T-1, same day)
* Follow-up with interested leads

---

## **3.4 Technical Setup**

* Setup primary and backup meeting links
* Test audio, video, and presentation
* Prepare attendance and feedback systems

---

# 🔵 **4. WORKSHOP EXECUTION PLAN (LIVE SESSION)**

---

## **4.1 Entry & Attendance**

* Participants join via meeting link
* Attendance captured through form

---

## **4.2 Content Delivery**

* Introduction (brand + agenda)
* Core AI concepts and tools
* Prompt engineering fundamentals
* Live AI demonstration

---
