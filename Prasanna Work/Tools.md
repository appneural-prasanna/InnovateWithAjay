# 🛠 Tools You Should Focus On (Based on Your Company)

## 📊 1. Tracking & Reports

* Use **Microsoft Excel**

  * Task tracking
  * KPI tracking
  * Daily/weekly reports

👉 This is your **most important tool**

---

## 📄 2. Documentation

* Use **Microsoft Word**

  * SOPs
  * Project plans
  * Reports

* Use **Microsoft OneNote**

  * Meeting notes
  * Ideas
  * Process notes

---

## 📋 3. Task & Project Management

* Use **Microsoft Planner** *(if available in your Microsoft 365)*
  OR
* Use **Microsoft Teams**

  * Assign tasks
  * Track progress
  * Team communication

---

## 💬 4. Communication

* **Microsoft Teams**

  * Daily updates
  * Team coordination
  * Meetings

---

## ⚙️ 5. Automation (Basic)

* Use **Microsoft Power Automate**

  * Automate approvals
  * Notifications
  * Task updates

---

## 🧑‍💻 6. GitHub (How You Use It in Ops)

* **GitHub**

  * Track developer tasks (issues/projects)
  * Monitor progress of technical work
  * Coordinate with tech team

👉 You don’t need coding—just **track status and updates**

---

# ⭐ Your Focus Priority

1. **Microsoft Excel** → Tracking & reports
2. **Microsoft Teams** → Communication & coordination
3. **Microsoft Word** → Documentation
4. **Microsoft Planner** → Task management
5. **Microsoft Power Automate** → Automation

---


# 🔥 One Line

**Use Excel to track, Teams to coordinate, Word to document, and Planner to manage tasks** 👍
